Project Report – Digital Communication Reflection 

In 2013, Justine Sacco, a PR executive, tweeted an offensive joke before boarding a long-haul flight to South Africa. By the time her plane landed, the tweet had gone viral under the hashtag #HasJustineLandedYet, she had been widely condemned online, and her employer had terminated her contract all before she had internet access to respond. This incident illustrates how a single moment of poor digital judgement can cause irreversible professional and reputational damage.

What could have been done differently? Quite simply the tweet should never have been sent. The core lesson is to pause before posting, especially on emotionally charged or sensitive topics. Applying a basic filter "Would I say this in a boardroom or classroom?" is often enough to prevent such errors. As Digital Ambassadors, it is our role to share exactly this kind of awareness with our peers.
